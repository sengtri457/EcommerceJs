Fit Tips Tall Tops Sizes (6’ – 6’5”/183-196cm): 1.75”/4.5cm longer in length than regular tops. Sleeve length is adjusted proportionately depending on silhouette. Tall sizes are only available for select styles. If you’re on the borderline between two sizes, order the smaller size for a tighter fit or the larger size for a looser fit. If your measurements for chest and waist correspond to two different suggested sizes, order the size indicated by your chest measurement.
